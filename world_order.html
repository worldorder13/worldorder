About this webpage
In order to better understand current events, it is necessary to have sufficient knowledge in geography, history, political science, economics, finance, psychology, sociology, critical thinking, logical fallacies, and cognitive biases. This webpage contains links to the essential books and skills that are necessary to better understand current events. There are also links to the most astute and colorful commentators and their opinions on the current events of the day.

Tip
Step 1) Use the essential books and essential skills to get a rough picture of the current world order and where it came from.
Step 2) Read current events and use the knowledge gained from step 1) to assess how current events are related to the current world order and how they affect it.
Step 3) Position yourself accordingly.
